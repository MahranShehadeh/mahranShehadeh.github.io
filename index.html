About

I’m a final‑year PhD researcher at the Weizmann Institute (Gilad Haran Lab). My work spans single‑molecule biophysics, nanophotonics, and scientific computing. I design plasmonic platforms (ZMWs, Au nanorods) to boost photon yields and build model‑free analysis that pulls kinetics directly from photon streams.

I’m passionate about clean, reproducible analysis and clear scientific communication. I’ve previously contributed to high‑energy physics (ATLAS, CERN) and enjoy bridging disciplines.
